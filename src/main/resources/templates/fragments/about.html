A Collaborative Photography Project

Snapshot Stories is a unique photography project that celebrates storytelling through a collaborative approach.

Here's how it works:
1. Each participant receives a camera from a friend.
2. They take a meaningful photo that includes both the giver (previous participant) and the receiver (current participant) of the camera.
3. Participants write a brief message about themselves and acknowledge the person who gave them the camera.
4. The camera is passed on to another person, who repeats the process.

This ongoing chain creates a collection of photos and personal stories, showcasing the diversity of human experiences. Snapshot Stories demonstrates how a simple camera can connect people and share stories within communities and beyond. Join us in celebrating the power of photography to bring people together.
